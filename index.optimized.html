About Us

Delivering Excellence in Precision Machining

Shree Hari Engineers is a precision machining partner specializing in HMC machining for complex, multi-face components. We support OEMs and manufacturers with repeatable quality, clear communication, and dependable delivery—from first prototype to full production.

We focus on what keeps your production on track: controlled tolerances, consistent surface finishes, inspection-ready parts, and lead times you can plan around.

Let's build more than parts—let's build long-term reliability.
